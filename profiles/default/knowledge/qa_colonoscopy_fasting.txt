Q: What solution do I need to drink for a colonoscopy?
A: You will drink the bowel prep prescribed for you. Your pharmacy can dispense it if we send the order electronically.
---
Q: Can you take a verbal order for prep?
A: Verbal orders take longer. Sending the prescription electronically is much faster.
---
Q: Is it normal to have loose or liquid stools after a colonoscopy?
A: Light changes in bowel movements can happen after a procedure. If symptoms worsen, contact your doctor.
---
Q: Do I need to drink a solution the day before?
A: Yes. The day before your procedure you must follow a clear-liquid diet and take your prescribed bowel prep.
---
Q: Do I really have to avoid eating the entire day before?
A: Yes. The day before your procedure is clear liquids only—no solid food.
---
Q: What counts as clear liquids?
A: Clear liquids include water, broth, clear juices without pulp, tea or coffee without milk, and clear Jell-O (no red or purple).
---
Q: When do I start clear liquids?
A: Clear liquids begin at midnight the day before your colonoscopy.
---
Q: Where does the bowel prep come from—pharmacy or Quest?
A: Your bowel prep is sent to your pharmacy. Quest does not provide the prep.
---
Q: The instructions say to start drinking at 2 pm—should I follow that?
A: Yes, follow the written instructions we provided, not the directions printed on the medication box.
