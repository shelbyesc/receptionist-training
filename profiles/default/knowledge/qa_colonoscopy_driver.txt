Q: If I do an upper endoscopy and a colonoscopy on the same day, will I need extra recovery time?
A: Not usually. The recovery time is generally the same whether you have one procedure or both on the same day.
---
Q: Do I need a driver for my colonoscopy?
A: Yes. Anyone receiving sedation must have a responsible adult drive them home.
---
Q: Can I drive myself home after a colonoscopy?
A: No. Because of the sedation, you will need someone to drive you home after the procedure.
