Q: How do I take the bowel prep?
A: Follow the written prep instructions we sent you, not the directions on the medication box. In general you drink the prep in two doses: the first the evening before your procedure, and the second early on the morning of the procedure. Drink it steadily along with the clear liquids listed in your instructions.
---
Q: When do I start drinking the prep?
A: Most patients begin the first dose the evening before the procedure, and the second dose the morning of. Your written instructions give the exact times for your prep — follow those rather than the box.
---
Q: I have a question about my colonoscopy preparation.
A: I can help with prep. The day before your procedure you follow a clear-liquid diet and take your prescribed bowel prep, following the written instructions we provided. If you have a question the instructions don't cover, please email the office and we'll get right back to you.
